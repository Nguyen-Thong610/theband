We love music

UNI5 is an idol boy group from Vietnam and debuted with 6 members in 2016. The known members of the group are Toki, Cody, Luc Huy, Tung Maru, Toof.P and K.O. Initially, the group debuted with only two members, Toki and K.O, on September 4, 2016 with the title song C'on. At the beginning of November 2017, UNI5's fanpage suddenly revealed the remaining 4 members: Cody, Luc Huy, Tung Maru and Toof.P.
However, in 2019, due to health reasons, leader Toki left the group. After that, UNI5 operated as 5 members. UNI5 members are all very talented and loved by many young Vietnamese. UNI5 pieces also possess a bright appearance and trendy fashion style. Each person has a different personality and style, but when combined, they create a perfect piece.
